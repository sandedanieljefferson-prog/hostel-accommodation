Help & FAQs

How to Use Hostelhub

  1. Browse Hostels: On the home page, use the search bar or dashboard menu to find available hostels.
  2. View Hostel Details: Click on any hostel to see its details, including location, distance, price, and room type.
  3. Book a Hostel: Click the "Book" button on your preferred hostel. You must be logged in to book.
  4. Login/Register: If you don’t have an account, register first. Use your credentials to log in.
  5. Bookings & Payments: After booking, go to the Bookings page to pay using mobile money options. Follow the instructions to complete payment.
  6. Real-Time Updates: If hostels are added, updated, or removed by the admin, your hostel list will update instantly. You’ll see a notification at the top of the page when this happens.
  7. Contact & Support: Use the Contact page to send messages to the admin. You’ll receive replies via your email, sometimes with downloadable files.
  8. Profile & History: Access your profile to view your booking and payment history, and to change your password.
  9. Dashboard Menu: Use the dashboard icon (top left) to access all pages and features.
  10. Logout: Use the dashboard menu to log out when finished.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Why can’t I book a hostel?
    A: You must be logged in to book. Please log in or register.
  • Q: How do I know my booking was successful?
    A: After booking, you’ll see it listed on your Bookings page. You can then proceed to payment.
  • Q: How do I pay for my booking?
    A: Go to the Bookings page and click "Pay". Follow the mobile money instructions.
  • Q: What if the hostel list changes while I’m browsing?
    A: The list will update automatically and you’ll see a notification.
  • Q: How do I contact support?
    A: Use the Contact page. The admin will reply to your email.
  • Q: Can I receive files from the admin?
    A: Yes, the admin can send you files via email with a download link.
  • Q: How do I update my profile?
    A: Go to your profile page from the dashboard menu.
  • Q: How do I log out?
    A: Use the dashboard menu and click "Logout".
  • Q: What if I forget my password?
    A: Use the password change option in your profile. If you need more help, contact support.
  • Q: What browsers are supported?
    A: Hostelhub works best on modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, and Edge.
